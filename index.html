What We Do

Comprehensive China sourcing services

Everything you need to source successfully from China — handled by someone physically on the ground.

01

Factory Finding & Vetting

In-person visits to factories across Shanghai, Shenzhen, Yiwu, Guangzhou and Ningbo. We verify credentials, certifications, and production capacity before connecting you.

02

Price Negotiation

On-the-ground negotiation to get you the most competitive prices without sacrificing quality. We know what fair factory prices look like — and we push for them.

03

Quality Control

Pre-shipment inspections with detailed QC reports and photos. Your goods are verified before they leave the factory — no surprises on arrival.

04

Sample Coordination

We arrange product samples so you can physically approve quality before committing to any bulk order — minimising risk and protecting your investment.

05

Shipping & Logistics

Full freight coordination from factory to your door. Sea or air freight, customs documentation, and delivery tracking — all managed for you.

06

Market Entry Strategy

Tailored e-commerce and market entry strategies for brands launching on Amazon, Shopify, or entering China's booming domestic market.
